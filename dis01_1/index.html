  4. How do you think a microprocessor is designed?

  5. What is the relationship between a microprocessor and assembly language?

  6. What are you expecting to learn from this class?

  7. The three types of instructions in LC-3 are ________, ________, and ________.

  8. In the LC-3, there are 8 ________________ that serve as on-chip memory.

  10. In the line of code BRnz LABEL, when would the branch be taken?

Problems

  1. What is 2 in binary? 4? 13? 27?

  2. What is 1001 0110 + 0011 1101? 0111 1111 + 0001 1100?

  3. What is -4 represented in 8-bit twos complement? -7? -18? -31? -117?

  4. What is the hexadecimal representation of all of the answers from the last question?

  5. Can -200 be represented in 8-bit twos complement?

  6. What is 0101 1001 - 0011 0111? 0011 1100 - 0101 1010?
